## COURSE OVERVIEW

Your student has been invited to participate in **AI Literacy Mastery**, an 8-session educational program designed to teach responsible and effective use of artificial intelligence tools. This course is part of our commitment to preparing students for a future where AI literacy is essential.

### What Students Will Learn

- How AI chatbots (like ChatGPT) actually work
- Ethical guidelines for using AI in academic work
- Critical evaluation of AI-generated content
- Creative applications: presentations, audio, video
- Study skills enhanced by AI tools
- College application boundaries and best practices

### Course Philosophy

**"AI as Creator, Student as Evaluator"**

Students learn to direct AI tools and critically assess their outputs—not to let AI do their thinking. This builds both technological fluency and irreplaceable human judgment.

## WHAT THIS MEANS FOR YOUR STUDENT

### During the Course

✅ **Will Do:**
- Create accounts on educational AI platforms
- Practice prompting AI tools with school-appropriate topics
- Generate and evaluate AI-created content
- Build a portfolio of projects using AI assistance
- Learn to disclose AI use transparently

❌ **Will NOT Do:**
- Enter personal information (name, address, etc.) into AI tools
- Use AI to complete graded assignments without disclosure
- Access AI tools without instructor supervision during class
- Share inappropriate content with AI systems

### Skills Developed

By course completion, your student will be able to:
1. Write effective prompts that get useful AI responses
2. Critically evaluate AI output for accuracy and bias
3. Use AI as a study and creativity partner
4. Understand when AI use is appropriate vs. inappropriate
5. Articulate their own Personal AI Use Charter

## DATA PRIVACY INFORMATION

### What Data Is Collected

When using AI tools, the following data may be processed:

| Data Type | Who Has Access | Retention |
|-----------|---------------|-----------|
| Text prompts | AI provider, instructor (if shared) | Varies by platform (30-90 days) |

### What We Do to Protect Privacy

- Students use school email accounts when available
- Students are instructed NEVER to enter personal identifying information
- All class activities are supervised by the instructor
- Students log out after each session
- No graded work is submitted directly to AI platforms

## ACADEMIC INTEGRITY

This course explicitly teaches the difference between appropriate and inappropriate AI use.

### What We Teach as APPROPRIATE:
- Using AI to brainstorm ideas
- Using AI to explain difficult concepts
- Using AI to check grammar after writing
- Using AI to create study materials
- Disclosing all AI assistance

### What We Teach as INAPPROPRIATE:
- Submitting AI-generated work as your own
- Using AI to write essays or complete assignments
- Hiding AI assistance from teachers
- Copying AI output without verification

**Students will sign a Personal AI Use Charter** at the end of the course committing to ethical AI use.
